About.me

My name is Pepijn Latour, at the moment I am studying ICT Media-Design at Fontys University of Applied Sciences. I like to research how people interpret things and how they look at a website, which is why I also want to immerse myself in UX design. Outside my school, I am a member of scouting Paulus, where I go two times a month for an activity or a spinning evening. Here I am also going to be treasurer of the rover scouts next season. On a shooting evening we often sit together with about eight people and then someone has made a program that we fill 2 hours with, then we have a drink. I also work at the Jan Linders in the fruit and vegetable department. In the future, I want to delve into UX design, because I think it would be interesting to investigate how people look at certain things. And how you can best keep people happy online.
