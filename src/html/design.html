SoundLab.

Assignment.

For this assignment we had to make a branding for the Fontys SoundLab.
The Stakeholder had a problem that the SoundLab was not known by the students or he atleast wanted it to be used more. so we created a brand and a awwareness campaign.

Logo.

we started creating diffenent logo's. eventually we put all the logo's in a survey and let the target audience make the decision. on the right you see the logo's i made. i made 3 logo's and asked josh wich one he liked most this one i put in the survey.

Style.choices.

My first logo is a more modern/minimalistic logo because it has no doodles or other random elements. I made this modern minimalistic because many people like the modern vibe. Here you see the element above the text. it represents a sound wave. This reconnects to the sound because there you can make sound.

For this design I went for a more playful vibe but still minimalistic. The lines at the top and by the exclamation mark make the logo more playful as well as the font the letters are rotated. I also brought a sound wave in the o from box as lines.

This is more of a warm and cozy design. It is a retro font. The colours make it more cozy and warm. I made a line above the retro text to connect the logo to the audio lab. This line represents a sound wave. This is also the logo that was chosen josh, So i chose this one to put in the survey.

When we took the survey we got 15 answers that said this is the most appealing logo. With this knowledge we know that the target audience wanted a retro/cozy vibe. here is a link to the survey answers: Survey
