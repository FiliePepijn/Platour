Communication.

In my previous group projects, our primary mode of communication was through Microsoft Teams, with teachers and group members, and we managed tasks by setting deadlines and maintaining a document to track what needed to be done. For urgent or delayed tasks, we relied on WhatsApp.
However, for my next group, I believe adopting a more structured task management system like Trello or using SCRUM methodologies would significantly better our way of working. This way I can ensure that no tasks are overlooked and make better task assigning. It would allow for multiple members to collaborate on the same task more effectively.
