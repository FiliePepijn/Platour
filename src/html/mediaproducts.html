Sprint.X

First.prototype

For sprint-X, I designed and coded a terminal-style portfolio website for Jasper Latour, a technology student in his second semester. Jasper wants his portfolio to feature a command line interface (CLI) for navigation. I started by designing the website in Adobe XD based on his preferences.
After presenting the initial design, Jasper wanted that the portfolio be accessible to others, so I added a hamburger menu for easier navigation.
After an interview, Jasper expressed a preference for a modern Linux terminal aesthetic with purple and gray colors and wanted a normal navbar. I researched modern Linux terminal designs and found the modern linux terminal colors, making the terminal a loose part of the website. All design iterations and interviews with Jasper are documented in the Adobe XD file and my research document. And for the final design in the Adobe XD, I made it interactive. Here you can find the full research document. If you want to see the final website, check it out at jasper.platour.net.
